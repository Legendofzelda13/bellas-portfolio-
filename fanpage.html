Maya Angelou

She was a writer, dancer, singer, and civils rights activist

Maya Angelou was born on april 4th, 1928 in St. Louis, Missouri and passed at 86 years old recently in 2014. As a young girl she loved writing and reading and also loved to write poems and later became an Author. As an African American it was hard for her to find jobs and most people still came across to the belief that we are not all equal so she became a civil rights activist. In 2010 She won the Presidential Medal of Freedom which was given to her by Mr. president obama for promoting peace and freedom.
